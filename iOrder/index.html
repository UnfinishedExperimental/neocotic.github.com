iOrder is an extension for Google Chrome that keeps users updated with their iDevice order progress.

If you're finding yourself constantly refreshing your order's page just install iOrder and it will do all the work for you and notify you when your order is updated. Also, iOrder provides easily accessible links to view and/or track your orders on the Apple store.
